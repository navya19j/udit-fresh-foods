About Us

Built on Grit, Grain & a Genuine Purpose

Udit Fresh Foods Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2007 by Mr. Nirmal Jain — a veteran with 25+ years spanning manufacturing, import-export, and supply chain. What began as a focused manufacturing unit has grown into one of India's most reliable co-packing operations.

We manufacture Roasted Peanuts, Sooji and Dalia, and co-pack a broad range of Groceries, Staples, Pulses, Dry Fruits, Cereals, Spices, Flours, Rice, Sugar and more. Our clients include national and multinational modern retailers, quick-commerce platforms, and emerging D2C grocery brands.

Quick-commerce veterans: We've been co-packing for Blinkit since it was still Grofers — we know what speed-to-shelf means.
Women-led on the floor: Over 80% of our workforce are women. This isn't a stat — it's a value we've held since day one.
Brand launchpad: We nurture budding entrepreneurs, giving them access to our full SKU catalogue and supply chain muscle to build their own grocery brand.

Our Process

How We Make Every Pack Count

A four-stage process that guarantees consistency whether you order 500 units or 5 million.

01

Ethically Sourced

100% clean, natural ingredients sourced from verified agricultural partners — traceable from farm to facility.

02

In-House Lab Testing

Every batch goes through our own laboratory — fumigation, sterilization, moisture checks, and quality validation before it moves forward.

03

GMP-Certified Packing

Processed and packed in hygienic, GMP-certified clean rooms — full traceability and tamper-proof packaging for every SKU.

04

Ready for Dispatch

Your branded, packed, and quality-passed goods — staged, labelled, and dispatched to your preferred fulfilment point on your timeline.

Partnership Options

Two Ways to Work With Us

Whether you want us to handle everything or stay in control of sourcing — we have a model for you.

Model A

Discounted Model

We Buy · We Pack · You Sell

We handle procurement end-to-end. You just sell.

  • We source raw materials directly from the market at the best rates
  • Every batch quality-tested and cleared before packing
  • Packed securely under your brand name and label
  • Invoiced to you at a structured discount on MRP — margin built in
Ideal for

New brands and quick-commerce sellers who want a completely managed, capital-light supply chain.

Model B

Cost Based Model

You Buy · We Pack · You Sell

You stay in control of sourcing. We bring the expertise.

  • Raw materials sourced jointly, in consensus with your buying team
  • Terms and pricing agreed upfront — no surprises
  • Same rigorous quality checks — conducted at our facility
  • Packed under your brand and dispatched on your schedule
Ideal for

Established brands and large retailers who want full visibility and control over their raw material sourcing.
